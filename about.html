About Waste2Worth

Waste2Worth is a circular waste marketplace built by a passionate group of students. Our goal is simple: turn waste into a valuable resource, and make recycling easier, smarter and more transparent.

Our Mission

We believe that every piece of waste has value when connected to the right people. Waste2Worth focuses on giving industries, small businesses and recyclers a trusted platform where they can buy, sell and repurpose materials responsibly. Our mission is to make sustainability accessible, profitable and user-friendly.
